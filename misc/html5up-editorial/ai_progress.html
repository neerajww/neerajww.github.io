Dataset Portals

Data Science needs data. You never know what kind of information you’ll find when analyzing a particular dataset. Sometimes it can be an eye-opener! To get started with your own analysis, you may find the below portals useful. Many of these host open-access datasets.

Global and National
  • Data.Gov.in, a portal by Government of India to access varied national datasets.
  • World Bank Open Data, by The World Bank.
  • Datahub Enterprise, by Datahub.
  • UN Data, by the United Nations.
  • Data.World, by Data World Organization.
  • The Humanitarian Data Exchange. Find, share and use humanitarian data all in one place, a service by OCHA.

Brain Imaging Dataset Portals
  • OpenNEURO. A free and open platform for validating and sharing BIDS-compliant MRI, PET, MEG, EEG, and iEEG data.
  • SWADESH, by NINS Lab, NBRC.

Speech and Text Dataset Portals
  • Open SLR, a Open Speech and Language Resources dataset.
  • The Spoken Wikipedia Corpora, by NatS, Uni. Hamburg.

Weather and Climate Dataset Portals
  • Berkeley Earth, time-series datasets to understand Global Warming.
  • Climate Data Service Portal, India a service by India Meteorological Department.
