Facilities

Recognized as a School to bring together and drive the data science & AI activities in IIT Guwahati, we have access to the following facilities.

  • Computer & Communication Center (CCC) Labs
  • High Performance GPU Computing station NVIDIA DGX A100 Server
  • Param-Ishan and Param-Kamrup Supercomputers for high performance computing
Besides above, our Faculty are also constantly working towards setting up equipments (and other resources) to pursue specialized research. Please have a look at their lab webpages to know more.

Founded in 2021, the School is currently in its early phase. The School is actively bringing together stakeholders to create the School infrastructure and state-of-the-art computing facilities.
