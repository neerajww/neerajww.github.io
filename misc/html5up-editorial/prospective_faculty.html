Why IIT Guwahati?

It has a Green-n-serene, 8000 plus students which includes 2000 plus PhD Scholars, well connected with major cities in India. The campus resides in a thriving community of intellectuals, bubbling with curiosity and passion. The Guwahati city acts as a door to the amazing geographical landscape of North-East India which features a rich bio-diversity. It has a strategic location, providing scope to define and work on challenging and unique global problems, and at the same time contribute to India's growth.
IIT Guwahati, established in 1994 as the sixth member of the IIT fraternity, is a prestigious technical institution in India known for its exceptional education and research standards. The campus boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure and a vibrant atmosphere, offering a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, sciences, design, and humanities. The institution focuses on providing students with a comprehensive education that encourages innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurship. By promoting interdisciplinary learning, cutting-edge research, and industry exposure, IIT Guwahati empowers students to excel in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to society. In the 'India Rankings 2023' announced by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) of the Union Ministry of Education, IIT Guwahati maintains its 7th position among India's top engineering institutions.
