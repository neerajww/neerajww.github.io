What is DS & AI @IIT-Guwahati?

Our vision is to establish ourselves as a place of academic excellence by imparting quality teaching, carrying out research and technology development in frontier areas of data science and knowledge engineering. We envisage to become a globally recognized place of excellence by adopting an interdisciplinary approach to research with contributions from the fields of computer science, web science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics, and humanities and social sciences.
  • To be a place for innovation, research and technology development in frontier areas of data science and AI.
  • To nurture the curious minds of scientists, undergraduate and postgraduate students, and build a talent pool ready to solve challenging problems with a Data Science and AI thought process.

Our People

DS & AI at IIT Guwahati unites students, faculty and staff from all areas of the university to create a well equipped group of AI researchers to design solutions for challenging problems.

Learn with Us

Our students work closely with faculty to tackle data science and AI challenges facing India and the world, in topics ranging from understanding molecular interactions in materials to building intelligent human-machine interaction systems.
