Time-scale Modification (TSM) Demo

Our proposed method is μTVS. The other methods are shown for comparison.

The details of the method can be learnt from the paper
Mel-scale sub-band modelling for perceptually improved time-scale modification of speech and audio signals,
published at the National Conference on Communications 2017, IEEE.
